About

Israel Alves is a guitarist with over 20 years of involvement in music, starting his journey at age 10 at the Sergipe State Conservatory of Music. With a strong foundation in classical guitar, he evolved into electric guitar in a self-taught way, developing his own identity. Influenced by Buckethead, Paul Gilbert and Steve Vai, his compositions combine technique, creativity and expressiveness. He also works in music production, recording, mixing and mastering, integrating art and technology.
